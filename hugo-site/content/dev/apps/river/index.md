**River** is a **decentralized group chat system** built on top of [Freenet](https://freenet.org). It features a modern web-based interface, ensuring seamless access from any device with a web browser.

---

## 💬 What Can You Do with River?

- **Chat** in public rooms (private rooms in the works)
- **Maintain privacy** — no email or phone number required (Note: River does not provide a complete anonymity layer, so advanced tracking methods could still identify you). 
- **Access messages** even after going offline
- **Control your identity** — no one can impersonate you
- **Use it without trusting any central authority**

---

## 🤔 Why Does River Exist?

Modern chat apps are fast and convenient — but they come with significant trade-offs. They often compromise your privacy, mine your personal data, and are vulnerable to censorship or shutdowns by centralized authorities.

**River exists to address these issues and give you back control**

- **No Central Servers**: Your messages aren’t stored by any company, so there’s no single point of failure or risk of surveillance.  
- **You’re in Control**: You own your identity and data without depending on big platforms.  
- **Always Connected**: River keeps working even if parts of the internet go down, thanks to its unique design.  
- **Built for Privacy**: River is designed to keep your chats private and secure, protecting your conversations from prying eyes.  
- **Ready for the Future**: River’s innovative design ensures it can adapt to the changing internet and keep your communication safe.  

River is more than just a chat app — it’s a step toward reclaiming your digital freedom in an increasingly centralized and surveilled online world.

## 🛠️ How to access River

1. **Download and Install Freenet**: Follow the instructions on the [Freenet website](https://freenet.org) to set up Freenet on your device.

2. **Create or Join a Room**:  
You can either create a new room or join an existing one using its contract address. To join a room you need an invitation from a current member and use it to access the room.  

3. **Choose Your Nickname**:  
Choose a nickname to represent yourself in the chat. Once set, you’re ready to start chatting and connecting with others!


## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is River free to use?
Yes, River is completely free to use. 

### How is my privacy protected?
River leverages Freenet’s decentralized and encrypted network to ensure your messages remain private and secure. No central servers mean no one can monitor or store your conversations.

### Can I use River on my phone or tablet?
Yes, River’s web-based interface works seamlessly on any device with a modern web browser, including phones, tablets, and desktops.

### What happens if I go offline?
River is designed to store messages locally, allowing you to access them even when you’re offline. Once you reconnect, the app will sync with the network to update your messages.

### Are private rooms available?
Private rooms are currently under development and will be available in a future update. Stay tuned for more features!

### Do I need an account to use River?
No, River doesn’t require an email address, phone number, or any traditional account setup.

### Is River Quantum-Safe?
River is implementing quantum-safe cryptographic algorithms to future-proof the security of user communications against potential quantum computing threats.
